What is Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang Studios, where players explore a blocky world made of blocks and can build, craft, and survive.
Minecraft's world is procedurally generated, meaning each new game creates a unique landscape to explore.

Who Made it?

Minecraft was created by Markus "Notch" Persson, a Swedish developer, in 2009. He founded Mojang to continue its development, and in 2014, the game and company were acquired by Microsoft.
Today, Minecraft is developed and maintained by Mojang Studios under Microsoft.
