Generated Structures

In Minecraft, procedural generation populates the world with unique structures—villages, temples, shipwrecks, and more—that reward exploration and discovery.

Village

Villages spawn in most Overworld biomes and are home to villagers who trade valuable items. You’ll find houses, farms, wells, and a bell at the center.

Desert Temple

Desert Temples appear in Desert biomes. Beneath the surface is a hidden chamber with four treasure chests—watch out for the pressure-plate trap!

Shipwreck

Shipwrecks drift in oceans and beaches. Each contains up to three chests with loot—and sometimes a treasure map pointing to buried riches.

Woodland Mansion

Rarest of Overworld structures, Woodland Mansions spawn in Roofed Forests. Inside, illagers roam secret rooms filled with loot and dangerous foes.
