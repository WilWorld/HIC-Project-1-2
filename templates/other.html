Minecraft Card Game

The game aims to be the first player to earn the required points, with 24 points in a 2 player game, 20 points in a 3 player game, and 16 points in a 4 player game.

Players shuffle resource and craft cards, and each player has a reserve card holder.

On their turn, players can perform mine, craft, or reserve actions.

Mine involves placing the top resource card from a resource pile, discarding a resource card if needed, and discarding a creeper if mined.

Craft involves discarding resource cards matching the craft card recipe, and each tool has a special ability that can be used once. Tool abilities include swords, shovels, pick axes, axes, hoes, and crafting items.

Reserves allow players to craft items at a later turn, but they cannot have a card already in their reserve.

The first player to reach the required number of points wins.
